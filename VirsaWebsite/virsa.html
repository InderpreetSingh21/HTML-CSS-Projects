Virsa: Our Tradition

Virsa: Our Tradition is a competitive dance team based in New York, dedicated to celebrating the vibrant heritage of Bhangra, a traditional folk dance from Punjab. Since 2006, Virsa has delivered energetic and dynamic performances that combine tradition with modern style. The team has competed at various events and showcased their talent through numerous local performances, captivating audiences with colorful attire, lively moves, and a passion for excellence. Committed to preserving their roots and inspiring new generations, Virsa celebrates culture, community, and joy with every performance.
